Custom Slipcovers

Refresh your furniture with a new look.

No need to throw away a piece of furniture when the fabric is worn or outdated as long as the frame is structurally sound. Transform your furniture with a new custom slipcover! The beauty of a slipcover is that it gives new life to an old piece of furniture, and even better, it can be easily removed for cleaning.

Slipcovers are made by pin-fitting, which results in a tight fit that looks close to being upolstered. I am happy to work with you on your fabric choice, whether it be a fabric from an outside source or one supplied by me. Most pieces can be done in my home studio and can be completed within a week.
